Data Scientist - Climate Analytics Platform

ClimateAI is on a mission to combat climate change through data-driven insights. We're seeking a passionate Data Scientist to join our climate analytics team and help organizations reduce their carbon footprint.

About ClimateAI:
We're building a platform that helps companies measure, track, and reduce their environmental impact. Our mission is to make sustainability actionable through data science and machine learning.

Role Overview:
You'll work on analyzing climate data, building predictive models for carbon emissions, and developing tools to help organizations make data-driven sustainability decisions.

Responsibilities:
- Process and analyze large climate datasets (GRIB, NetCDF formats)
- Build machine learning models for emissions forecasting
- Develop algorithms for carbon footprint calculation
- Work with scientific computing libraries (NumPy, Pandas, SciPy)
- Collaborate with climate scientists and engineers
- Create data visualizations and dashboards
- Handle geospatial data and climate model outputs

Requirements:
- 3+ years of data science or scientific computing experience
- Strong Python skills with scientific libraries
- Experience with climate data or environmental science
- Knowledge of machine learning and statistical modeling
- Familiarity with NetCDF, GRIB, or HDF5 file formats
- Experience with data processing on HPC environments
- Understanding of geospatial analysis

Nice to have:
- Background in environmental science or meteorology
- Experience with R for statistical analysis
- Knowledge of renewable energy systems
- Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP)
- Familiarity with climate models (CMIP, ERA5)

Why join us:
- Make a real impact on climate change
- Work with cutting-edge climate data
- Collaborate with domain experts
- Purpose-driven mission and team
- Competitive salary and benefits

Location: Remote or Berlin, Germany
Compensation: $90k-$130k + equity + benefits
